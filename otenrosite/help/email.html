E-mail Settings

Set the email address that sends order confirmation emails to customers

How to set the email address that sends order confirmation emails to customers?

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Email Settings”
  3. Enter the email address you would like to send emails to your customers from in the “From Email”
  4. Click “Next”
  5. Enter the “Domain Name” for example of your email address example@otenro.com, you must enter www.otenro.com in the domain field.
  6. Enter your “User Name”. Generally, your user name will be your email address.
  7. Enter the “Password” you use to access the email account
  8. Click “Save”

Define the reply to address for your emails

How to define the reply to address for your emails

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Email Settings”
  3. Enter the email address you would like to set as your reply to address in the “Reply to Email” field
  4. Click “Save”

Set the logo for emails sent to customers

How to set the logo for emails sent to customers

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Email Settings”
  3. Navigate to “Header & Footer” from the tabs at the top of the window or by clicking “Next”
  4. Upload your logo to “Insert Header Image”

Send a test Email

How to send a test Email?

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Email Settings”
  3. Navigate to “Order Confirmation Email” from the tabs at the top of the pop up or click “Next”
  4. Click “Send Test Email”
