Inventory Settings

Define low inventory alert level

How to define low inventory alert level?

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Email Settings”
  3. Enter the percentage level at which when a product reaches that level you would like to be alerted at in the “Alert At” field

Delete a product

How to delete a product

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Inventory”
  3. You can search for a particular product by using the search bar or by going through the order list.
  4. Click on the “Delete icon”
  5. Click on “Yes” to confirm you would like to delete the product

Delete a product variant

How to delete a product variant

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Inventory”
  3. You can search for a particular product by using the search bar or by going through the order list
  4. Click on the “+” icon
  5. Click on the “Delete icon” on the variant you would like to delete
  6. Click on “Yes” to confirm you would like to delete the product

Edit the quantity of a product

How to edit the quantity of a product

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Inventory”
  3. You can search for a particular product by using the search bar or by going through the order list
  4. Click on the “+” icon
  5. Edit the quantity
  6. Click on “Save”

Edit the Price of a product

How to edit the Price of a product

  1. Navigate to “Commerce”
  2. Select “Inventory”
  3. You can search for a particular product by using the search bar or by going through the order list
  4. Click on the “+” icon
  5. Edit the price to the desired price for each variant
  6. Click on “Save”
