Payment Settings

Cash Payment

To enable "Cash On Pickup"

  1. Navigate to Commerce
  2. Select “IPG Integration”
  3. In the popup window toggle the "Cash on pickup" switch to the ON position

To enable "Cash On Delivery"

  1. Navigate to Commerce
  2. Select “IPG Integration”
  3. In the popup window toggle the "Cash on Delivery" switch to the ON position

Paypal

How to enable Paypal on your app

  1. Navigate to Commerce
  2. Select “IPG Integration”
  3. In the popup window toggle the "Paypal" switch to the ON position and follo the steps below
  4. Visit https://developer.paypal.com/developer/applications/ . Create a Paypal account if you do not already have one
  5. Click on “My Apps and Credentials”
  6. Under the Rest API section scroll down and click on the “Create App” Button
  7. Enter the App Name. This should be the same as what you have named the App in Otenro
  8. Click on Live toggle button
  9. If you are prompted to upgrade to “Business or Premier Account” please proceed and do so.
    Once that is completed please navigate back to this page
  10. Once you have navigated back to this page copy the Client ID
  11. Enable the PayPal by toggling the switch
  12. Paste the client in the Client ID field below the PayPal toggle switch

Stripe

How to enable Stripe on your app

  1. Navigate to Commerce
  2. Select “IPG Integration”
  3. In the popup window toggle the "Stripe" switch to the ON position and follo the steps below
  4. Navigate tohttps://dashboard.stripe.com/
  5. Login to your stripe account or sign up and create an account
  6. Go to “Your Account” menu and select “Account Settings”
  7. Select “API Keys” tab in the window
  8. Copy the “Live Secret Key” value from the dialog
  9. Toggle the Stripe switch into the on position
  10. Paste the “Live Secret Key” in the Otenro IPG settings section into the Stripe API Key field
